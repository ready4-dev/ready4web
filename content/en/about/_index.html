ready4 is being implemented by Monash University Health Economics Group. The project lead is Matthew Hamilton.

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}}

Catalyst

In July 2017, with funding support from VicHealth, Orygen and Victoria University, we began to plan a modular and open source health economic model of resilience and mental wellbeing in young people called readyforwhatsnext.

{{% /blocks/lead %}} {{% blocks/lead color="white"%}}

Current

We are currently developing the ready4 software framework for enable us to implement readyforwhatsnext. We are pursuing these goals by an Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) Scholarship funded PhD project and through multiple independently funded and governed modelling projects.
